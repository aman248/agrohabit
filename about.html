About us

Agrohabit™ is one of the fastest-growing start-ups in the Agri Tech sector one of the very few companies providing end-to-end solutions and services to the farming community in India. We are building AI-enabled technologies to revolutionize supply chain and production efficiency in the farm sector. Currently, we are operating in - Rajasthan, Punjab and Haryana - with 6000 farmers in our service network and our goal is to bring our services to 1 million farmers by 2025.
