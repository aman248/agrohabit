Cashews

Cashew Nuts Are The Dry Fruits Separated From The Kernels Of Cashew Nut Fruit By Boiling And Roasting Method. They Are Half Moon Shaped With Dicotyledon Nuts Colored Whitish To Dark Whitish. They Are More Delicious Used In Cooking, Baking, Dressing The Foods. The Consumption Of Nuts Have Multi Health Benefits Because Of Having Rich Fiber, Heart Healthy Fats And Proteins.
