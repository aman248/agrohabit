Contract Farming

Agrohebit has brought the contract farming of Anjeer for your growth and development. In which Agrohebit provides you high quality fig plants and buys the fruit and Farmers are provided with all kinds of technical assistants so that they can produce more at a lower cost. Anjeer gardening No special type of land is required for fig cultivation, it can be done in all types of land and water. Anjeer plant is prepared with tissue technique, it can tolerate more cold and heat and there is very less disease outbreak. The time of planting is February, March and July, August, September. This plant performs well in less fertilizer and water. The fig size of this variety is large and weighs on average 60-90 grams. The production of a two-year-old plant is 7-10 kg on an average, this production keeps increasing every year.

Soil Test

Knowing the Soil is where it all starts – the nutrient content and chemical composition of the soil defines how fertile the soil is and what level of plant life it can support and sustain on a consistent basis. We help our farmers get the soil test done at our testing labs or bring in their existing soil health cards. Soil composition data points provide foundations for selecting the right kind of Agri Input (seed/fertilizer) and to generate advanced analytics e.g. yield predictions and Personalized Advisory.

AI, ML & Analytics
Quantity, quality and availability of farm produce are important factors that drive major part of India’s economy. It has a direct impact on livelihood of the farmers as well as operations of downstream food production companies.


Using Data Science, Agri Science and Machine Learning technologies, we have been working on Artificial Intelligence engine that can correlate many of parameters that affect agriculture and provide predictive analytics and early warning solutions for better production and prediction.
